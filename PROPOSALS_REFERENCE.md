# Tower Networking Inc - Complete Proposals Reference

This document contains all in-game proposals with their details, prerequisites, costs, and effects.

## Technology & Research Proposals

### Remote Backups
- **Lore:** "3-2-1, let's back it up!"
- **Cost:** 450
- **Prerequisites:**
  - Technology must not already be acquired
- **Description:** Adds a new "sftp" routine to netshell. Allows backup of configs/files on remote devices for price of 450. The routine can also be used to remove malware when regular program uninstalls do not work.

---

### Botnets Research
- **Lore:** "Not enough users?"
- **Cost:** 665
- **Prerequisites:**
  - Technology must not already be acquired
  - At least 5 compute servers owned (increases selection chance)
- **Description:** Adds a new "botconf" routine to netshell. Allow configuration of bots that can generate visitor traffic to enterprises. Funding costs 665.

---

### High Availability Research
- **Lore:** "HA HA HA, no DR!"
- **Cost:** 600
- **Prerequisites:**
  - Technology must not already be acquired
  - At least 7 network routers owned (increases selection chance)
- **Description:** Adds a new "haconf" routine to netshell. Allow configuration of high-availability setup on ha-enabled routers. Funding costs 600.

---

### Jailbreaker
- **Lore:** "Hardware liberation day"
- **Cost:** 1,000
- **Prerequisites:**
  - Technology must not already be acquired
  - Remote Backups must be submitted
- **Description:** The "sftp" routine can now be used to install programs extracted from devices. Costs 1000.

---

### NetOps Research
- **Lore:** "Improvise, adapt, overcome"
- **Cost:** 330
- **Prerequisites:**
  - Technology must not already be acquired
- **Description:** Adds automation utility for managing and monitoring your network for the price of 330. Unlocks the 'cron', 'try' and 'notify' routines on NetShell.

---

### Power Management Research
- **Lore:** "Keep bills low and reliability high."
- **Cost:** 225
- **Prerequisites:**

## Tenabolt Corporation Proposals

**Note:** The mutual exclusivity between these proposals is **one-way only**. Choosing Legal Retaliation or Lobby Against will block Fusion Plant proposals from appearing. However, choosing Fusion Plant proposals does NOT prevent Legal Retaliation or Lobby Against from appearing later (this appears to be a bug).

### Fusion Plant Funding (Phase 1)
- **Lore:** "Let's make a Sun."
- **Cost:** 1,000
- **Prerequisites:**
  - Must NOT have chosen Legal Retaliation
  - Must NOT have chosen Lobby Against
- **Description:** Support Tenabolt Corporation's effort in building a fusion power plant by funding 1000. Reduce all Data Center power cost by 20.0%

---

### Fusion Plant Funding (Phase 2)
- **Lore:** "Let's make it brighter."
- **Cost:** 2,000
- **Prerequisites:**
  - Fusion Plant Funding (Phase 1) must be submitted
  - Must NOT have chosen Legal Retaliation
  - Must NOT have chosen Lobby Against
- **Description:** Support Tenabolt Corporation's effort in building a fusion power plant by funding 2000. Reduce all Data Center power cost by 50.0%. Reduces probability of power outage/surge by 10.0%

---

### Legal Retaliation
- **Lore:** "Another power outage? See you in court!"
- **Cost:** None (merchant pricing increases)
- **Prerequisites:** None 
- **Description:** Lawyer up against Tenabolt Corporation. This proposal eliminates future collaboration with Tenabolt Corporation. Tenabolt Corporation now pays penalties per outage/surge events. All items sold by Tenabolt reseller become more expensive.

---

### Lobby Against Tenabolt Corporation
- **Lore:** "Power to the people."
- **Cost:** None (merchant pricing increases)
- **Prerequisites:** None
- **Description:** Lobby for new law against Tenabolt Corporation. This proposal eliminates future collaboration with Tenabolt Corporation. Tenabolt Corporation can no longer issue fines against non Data Center power use. All items sold by Tenabolt reseller become more expensive.
